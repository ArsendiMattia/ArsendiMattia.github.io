I want to compute $ \nabla^2_{\boldsymbol{\theta}} \log p( \boldsymbol{\theta} \mid D) $, where $\boldsymbol{\theta} = (\theta_1, \theta_2, ..., \theta_n)$ and $D$ represents the observed data. 
$$
\begin{equation}
  \log p( \boldsymbol{\theta} \mid D) = \log p( D \mid \boldsymbol{\theta}) + \log p(\boldsymbol{\theta})
\end{equation}
$$
